Welcome to Phonetic Translator
Your very own PT!

Phonetic translator is a tool that helps users with with their pronunciations of words in either english or spanish.

Users have the option to get phonetics in the language of their choice!

Follow these steps to use the app:

1. Pick an input mode!

Use the toggle on the right of the screen to pick an input mode!

2. Write an input in your chosen language!

3. Pick an output!

Pick an output at the bottom of the screen! Outputs in English and Spanish are available when english is selected in step 1!

4.Get your Phonetics!

Your requested phonetics will apear to the screen!

5.Listen or Download your phonetics!

Use to listen and to download your phonetics located on the right of the screen!
